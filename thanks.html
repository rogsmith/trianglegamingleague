We thought it was time to take
legal work out of email.

When going through the process of selling a company, we felt first hand the pain that many lawyers must feel on a daily basis. Sending emails back and forth amongst internal team members and to other parties when trying to come to deal terms, keeping track of document versions, redlines, handling comments etc. We thought there has to be a better way to do this where all parties both internal and otherwise can easily see where a project stands at any given time.
