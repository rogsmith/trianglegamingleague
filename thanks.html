Thank You!

Enjoy the thrill of playing eSports with your friends against other teams with weekly, live, in-person matchups.

We are taking the idea of eSports tournaments, but turning them into a full 6 - 8 week season of weekly matchups against other teams. Join us at our local venue to battle against other squads. We'll schedule the matchups, the venue and the equipment. We will even have different levels so no matter if you are a beginner or expert you will find fair competition.

Leagues

Questions

How does it work?

A season will be anywhere from 6 - 8 weeks. Each season will consist of weekly matchups against other teams at our arena. You and your team show up for your weekly matchups against other teams competing in the same arena.

How is it different than a tournament?

Unlike tournaments where you have to qualify, here anyone can join. Also unlike tournaments where you play for 1-day, with our league you play each week against other teams all in the same arena.

Where is the arena located?

We are still working on the exact venue, but it will likely be somewhere in Cary or Morrisville, NC.

How much will it cost?

Each player will pay $80 to compete for a full-season. Each season will be between 6 - 8 weeks and will consist of weekly games.
